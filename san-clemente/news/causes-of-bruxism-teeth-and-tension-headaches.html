Causes of Bruxism Teeth


Bruxism teeth and tension headaches can be caused by many things. From stress, to genetics (or heredity), the causes of bruxism are varied! Furthermore, poor posture can also be a factor in causing these issues. Additionally, many individuals will have trouble sleeping due to the pain associated with the disorder.
